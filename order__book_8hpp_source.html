19  * Maintain the ITCH-5.0 order book for a single security.
20  *
21  * ITCH-5.0, like other market data feeds provide order-by-order
22  * detail, that is, the feed includes a message for each order
23  * received by the exchange, as well as the changes to these
24  * orders, i.e. when the execute, when their quantity (and/or price)
25  * is modified, and when they are canceled.. Such feeds are sometimes
26  * called Level III feeds. Typically only orders that do not
27  * immediately execute in full are included in the feed.
28  *
29  * There is substantial variation in the format of the messages, and
30  * some variation as to whether executions are represented differently
31  * than a partial cancel, and whether changes in price are allowed or
32  * create new order ids.
33  *
34  * This class receives a stream of (unnormalized) ITCH-5.0 messages
35  * for a single security, and organizes the orders in a
36  * book, i.e. a data structure where orders at the same price are
37  * grouped together, and one can quickly ask:
38  *
39  * - What is the best bid (highest price of BUY orders) and what is
40  * the total quantity available at that price?
41  * - What is the best offer (lowest price of SELL orders) and what is
42  * the total quantity available at that price?
43  */
